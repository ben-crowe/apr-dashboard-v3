Hypothetical Conditions

The use of a hypothetical condition(s) may have impacted the results of the assignment. The As Stabilized value has been developed in part based on the hypothetical condition that all units could achieve current market rent levels and has achieved stabilized occupancy as of the effective date of the appraisal. Under this premise, no additional rental income concessions (such as free rent, move-in specials, or the like) are provided. In reality, as of the effective date, the property's existing lease terms reflect contract rents that are deemed to be below-market rents. For the purposes of this analysis, it is assumed that lease-up to market rent levels has occurred under typical market conditions, without undue delay or concessions exceeding market norms. If this assumption proves incorrect, such as market rents are not achievable the value conclusion may be materially impacted.
