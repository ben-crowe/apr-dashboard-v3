- **Atomic commits with Valcre reference**: `feat(fields): add [field-id] with Valcre mapping [Named_Range]`
- **Use existing directory structure**: Don't create new field management folders, use `/2-Field Management/`

- **Kebab-case standard**: All lowercase with hyphens, no underscores
- **Valcre patterns**: `IA_DirCap_*`, `SA1_N_*`, `SU1_N_*`, `Subject_*`, `Value_*`
