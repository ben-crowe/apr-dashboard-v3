# Add Gmail Scope

**Found:** "Add or Remove Scopes" button

---

## Step 1: Click "Add or Remove Scopes"

**Click the button** - it will open a dialog or page

---

## Step 2: Search for Gmail Scope

**In the search/filter box, type:**
- `gmail.readonly`

---

## Step 3: Select the Scope

**Find this scope in the list:**
- `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly`

**Check the box** next to it

---

## Step 4: Save

**Click:**
- "Update" or "Save" or "Add" button

---

**Action:** Click "Add or Remove Scopes", search for `gmail.readonly`, check the box, and save!
