Valta Property Valuations Ltd. is proud to present the appraisal report that satisfies the agreed upon scope of work with 1021098645 Saskatchewan Ltd. The purpose of this assignment is to provide the As Stabilized current market value of the subject property, which at the time of inspection represents the improved property as of the effective date and is leased up at market rental rates and operating costs for the property located at 1101, 1121 109 St, North Battleford, SK (herein after referred to as the subject property).

The subject property, located at 1101, 1121 109 St, North Battleford, SK, is a multi-family, walkup property with improvements located in North Battleford. The improvements are comprised of 2 total buildings, and consist of 19,204 square feet of rentable area (NRA) as of the valuation date. The property, reportedly built in 1970 (1970 weighted) is approximately 100.0% occupied and features 16 units in a 2-story, garden style format.

Hypothetical Conditions

The use of a hypothetical condition(s) may have impacted the results of the assignment. The As Stabilized value has been developed in part based on the hypothetical condition that all units could achieve current market rent levels and has achieved stabilized occupancy as of the effective date of the appraisal. Under this premise, no additional rental income concessions (such as free rent, move-in specials, or the like) are provided. In reality, as of the effective date, the property's existing lease terms reflect contract rents that are deemed to be below-market rents. For the purposes of this analysis, it is assumed that lease-up to market rent levels has occurred under typical market conditions, without undue delay or concessions exceeding market norms. If this assumption proves incorrect, such as market rents are not achievable the value conclusion may be materially impacted.
