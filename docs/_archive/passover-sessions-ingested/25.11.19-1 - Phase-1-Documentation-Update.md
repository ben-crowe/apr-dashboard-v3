### Payment Flow (10 Steps)

1. **Client signs LOE** via DocuSeal portal
2. **DocuSeal webhook** fires to APR Dashboard (already working)
3. **Time delay** (configurable, e.g., 1 hour) - allows client to review signed LOE
4. **Email 2: Thank You + Payment Request** sent automatically
5. **Client clicks "Pay Now"** → Redirects to branded payment page
6. **Payment page** opens (Valta.ca branding, similar design to website)
7. **Client enters payment details** via Stripe Checkout
8. **Payment processed** by Stripe
9. **Stripe webhook** fires to APR Dashboard with payment confirmation
10. **Automatic updates triggered:**
    - Job status: "payment_received" → "active"
    - ClickUp task status: "Payment Requested" → "Active - In Progress"
    - Payment confirmation email sent to client
    - Team notification (email/Slack): "Job VAL##### activated"

## Key Learnings

### 1. Documentation Audience Matters

**Learning:** Technical documentation for build team should NOT include business metrics, ROI calculations, or sales language

**Application:** Keep docs focused on WHAT to build, HOW to build it, and WHEN to build it - not WHY from a business perspective

---

### 2. Previous System Knowledge is Valuable

**Learning:** The 10-folder Box structure had good reasons for existing - more granular organization than simplified modern approach

**Application:** Don't simplify existing systems without understanding their usage - "old school" folder organization can coexist with modern search/tagging

**User Quote:** "Lots of folders, old school, ten types of searches is the soon-to-be modern"

---

### 3. Dual Naming Conventions Enable Both Systems

**Learning:** Can maintain consistency between systems with different naming requirements by using folder numbers

**Application:**
- Supabase: URL-friendly short names (`5-client-provided`)
- Google Drive: Descriptive full names (`5. Client Provided Information`)
- Folder numbers (1-10) create searchable taxonomy across both

---

### 4. Payment Acceleration Has Cash Flow Impact

**Learning:** Collecting retainers same-day vs 3-7 days has significant cash flow benefits beyond just time savings

**Application:** Payment automation isn't just about convenience - it's about revenue acceleration and working capital improvement
