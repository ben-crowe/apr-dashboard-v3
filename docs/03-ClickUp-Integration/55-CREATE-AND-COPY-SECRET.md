# Create and Copy Client Secret - Step by Step

**Important:** You must copy the secret IMMEDIATELY after creating it!

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Create New Secret

1. **On the OAuth client page**, look for:
   - "ADD SECRET" button
   - Or "Create Secret" button
   - Usually near the "Client secrets" section

2. **Click:** "ADD SECRET" or "Create Secret"

---

### Step 2: Copy Secret IMMEDIATELY

**Right after clicking, Google will show you the secret:**

1. **A dialog/popup will appear** showing the new secret
2. **The secret will look like:** A long string of random characters
3. **DO THIS IMMEDIATELY:**
   - **Select all the text** (the secret)
   - **Copy it** (Cmd+C on Mac, Ctrl+C on Windows)
   - **Paste it somewhere safe** (like a text editor or notes app)
   - **DO NOT close the dialog** until you've copied it!

---

### Step 3: Share the Secret

**Once you've copied it:**

1. **Paste it here in the chat** (I'll create the JSON file for you)
2. **Or save it** and I'll help you create the file

---

## What the Secret Looks Like

The secret will be a long string, something like:
- `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45pqr678`
- Or similar format (random characters)

---

## Important Notes

- ⚠️ **The secret is ONLY shown ONCE**
- ⚠️ **After you close the dialog, you CANNOT see it again**
- ⚠️ **Copy it BEFORE clicking "Done" or closing**

---

**Ready?** Click "ADD SECRET", then immediately copy the secret and paste it here!
