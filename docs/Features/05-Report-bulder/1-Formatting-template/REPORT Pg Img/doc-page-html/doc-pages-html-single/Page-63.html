Under this premise, no deductions are made for holding costs, rent loss, or lease-up expenses. In addition to a hypothetical condition that all units could achieve current market rents levels and stabilized occupancy as of the effective date. For purposes of this analysis, it is assumed that lease-up to market rent levels has occurred and typical market conditions, without undue delay or concessions exceeding market norms. If this assumption proves incorrect, such as market rents are not achievable, the value conclusion may be materially impacted.
