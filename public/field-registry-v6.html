About Narratives
What are narratives?
Auto-generated legal text inserted into appraisal reports and LOE documents. Each narrative is triggered by a specific field value in the cascade logic.
How do they connect to the cascade?
When Value Scenarios is set to a specific outcome (e.g. "As If Vacant Land"), the system looks up matching narratives and inserts them into the report. The trigger field determines which text appears.
What's next?
Chris is building out the Text Library with additional types: Hypothetical Conditions, scope statements, market condition narratives, and more. Each will follow the same pattern — triggered by field values, auto-inserted into documents.
