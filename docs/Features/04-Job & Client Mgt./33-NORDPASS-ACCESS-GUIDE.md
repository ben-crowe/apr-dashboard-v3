# NordPass Access Guide

**Purpose:** How to access credentials shared via NordPass  
**Date:** January 8, 2026

## 🔍 What is NordPass?

**NordPass** is a password manager (similar to LastPass) made by NordVPN. It allows secure sharing of passwords and credentials.

**Key Points:**
- ✅ Password manager (like LastPass)
- ✅ Can share credentials securely
- ✅ Requires NordPass account to receive shared items
- ❌ NOT an authenticator app (that's different)

---

## 📧 How NordPass Sharing Works

When Chris shares credentials via NordPass, you typically receive:

### Option 1: Email Invitation
- **Email from:** NordPass or Chris
- **Subject:** Something like "Chris shared credentials with you"
- **Contains:** Link to accept shared credentials
- **Action:** Click link → Create NordPass account (if needed) → Accept credentials

### Option 2: Secure Link
- **Email contains:** Secure link to view credentials
- **Action:** Click link → View credentials (may require NordPass account)

### Option 3: Direct Share
- **If you have NordPass:** Credentials appear in "Shared Items" section
- **Location:** NordPass app → Shared Items → Pending shares

## ✅ How to Access Shared Credentials

### Step 1: Check Your Email

1. **Check your email inbox** (`bc@crowestudio.com`)
2. **Look for email from:**
   - NordPass
   - Chris (mentioning NordPass)
   - Subject might say "shared credentials" or "NordPass"

### Step 2: Create NordPass Account (If Needed)

**If you don't have NordPass:**

1. **Go to:** https://nordpass.com
2. **Click:** "Sign Up" or "Get Started"
3. **Create account** with your email (`bc@crowestudio.com`)
4. **Download app** (optional but recommended):
   - Desktop app: https://nordpass.com/download/
   - Browser extension: Available for Chrome, Firefox, etc.
   - Mobile app: iOS/Android

### Step 3: Accept Shared Credentials

**Via Email Link:**
1. Click the link in the email from Chris/NordPass
2. Log into NordPass (or create account)
3. Click "Accept" to add credentials to your vault

**Via NordPass App:**
1. Open NordPass app
2. Go to **"Shared Items"** section
3. Look for **"Pending"** shares
4. Click on shared item
5. Click **"Accept"**

## 🆚 NordPass vs LastPass

**Similarities:**
- Both are password managers
- Both can share credentials
- Both have browser extensions
- Both have mobile apps

**Differences:**
- **NordPass:** Made by NordVPN, newer
- **LastPass:** More established, you're already using it

**You can use both:**
- Keep LastPass for your personal passwords
- Use NordPass just for shared credentials from Chris
- Or import NordPass credentials into LastPass (if possible)
