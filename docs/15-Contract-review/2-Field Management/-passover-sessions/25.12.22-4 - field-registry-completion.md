## 🎓 Key Learnings

### 1. Naming Convention Consistency
**Issue:** Initially added fields with concatenated names (`caprate-high`) instead of full kebab-case (`cap-rate-high`).

**Lesson:** Always verify naming convention against template before bulk additions. Full kebab-case is the standard: every word separated by hyphens.

### 2. Template Alignment Validation
**Issue:** First verification showed 100% but actually had 10 mismatches due to naming.

**Lesson:** Template comparison must check exact field ID strings, not just presence/absence. String matching is case-sensitive and hyphen-sensitive.

### 3. Systematic Addition Strategy
**Success:** Breaking 119 fields into 4 priorities allowed:
- Focused work (comparable groups together)
- Easier verification per batch
- Clearer commit messages
- Simpler rollback if issues found

**Lesson:** Large-scale additions benefit from logical grouping by section/subsection.
